Surface container for content cards, list rows and panels.

```jsx
<Card interactive as="a" href="#">
  <h3>Application #4821</h3>
  <p>Submitted 12 May · Stockholm</p>
</Card>

<Card navy>
  <h3>Building better public services</h3>
</Card>
```

- `padded` (default true), `flat` (no shadow), `interactive` (hover lift), `navy` (dark variant)
- compose freely — Card is a styled surface, not a rigid template
