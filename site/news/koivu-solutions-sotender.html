February 23, 2026 Acquisition

PubliX Group partners with Koivu Solutions (Sotender)

A modern SaaS solution for connecting healthcare workers with open shifts, increasing reach and reducing costs for healthcare providers.

PubliX Group is building a leading platform of vertical SaaS companies serving the public sector across Northern Europe. Through our latest strategic move, we are strengthening our position and expanding our ability to deliver mission-critical software solutions to municipalities, regions, and public authorities.

"We are thrilled to announce this next step in our journey," said Alexander Hübel, Group CEO of PubliX Group. "This aligns perfectly with our vision to empower the public sector with the very best digital tools available on the market."

With this addition, the group continues to demonstrate its commitment to long-term value creation, focusing on companies that share a deep understanding of their end-users' daily realities and regulatory requirements.

About Koivu Solutions

Koivu Solutions developed Sotender to revolutionize healthcare procurement in Finland. They are now expanding across the Nordics.
