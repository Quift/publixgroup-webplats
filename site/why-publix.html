What does the journey look like?

From first call to day one in 3–5 months

No surprises, no hidden agendas. Here’s exactly what happens, step by step.

1. First conversation

~1 hour

A relaxed, confidential conversation between you and our leadership. No PowerPoints, no pressure. We want to understand your company, your journey and what you’re thinking about. You’ll get a clear sense of who we are and how we work.

Alexander Hübel, CEO
Marcus Åström, Head of M&A

2. Getting to know each other

2–4 weeks

We sign a mutual NDA and start learning about your business in more depth — financials, customers, product, team. You learn more about us. This is a two-way evaluation, and we take it seriously.

3. Value creation plan & indicative offer

~2 weeks

We present a tailored value creation plan — how your company could scale through new markets, shared customers, AI capabilities and reduced technical debt as part of PubliX. Alongside it, you receive an indicative offer with transparent valuation methodology. No complex structures.

4. Due diligence

4–8 weeks

A structured process where we verify the details together. We use experienced advisors and keep the process respectful of your time. Most founders say this is where they realised we were different — we treat DD as collaboration, not interrogation.

5. Welcome to PubliX

Day one

You join a group of founder-led companies building for the public good. Your team and, your office — all stay. What changes: you get shared AI capabilities, AI automation of internal processes, cross-selling opportunities, and colleagues who understand your market.
