About PubliX Group

Operators building for the public good

PubliX Group unites founder-led SaaS companies that digitise public services across Northern Europe. We're operators — not financial engineers. Every decision we make starts with one question: does this help our companies build better products?

Our thesis

Public-sector software is its own discipline

Public sector means procurement rules, framework agreements, long sales cycles and regulated buyers. It requires patience, domain expertise and a deep understanding of how the public sector actually buys technology. PubliX concentrates in this one vertical — and intends to be the best in the Nordics. When a founder joins PubliX, they get a parent company that truly understands their market.

We grow through shared customers, not cost-cutting. Our companies sell to the same and adjacent customers — municipalities, regions, foundations, public agencies. Cross-selling and collaboration between portfolio companies grows revenue organically, rather than stripping cost or merging teams. Every company keeps its identity, its team and its autonomy.

How we create value

Two tracks. One goal: make your company stronger.

Every company we work with benefits from two parallel value creation tracks — one focused on strengthening your foundation, the other on accelerating growth.

Foundations

Strengthen the operational core so you can scale with confidence.

  • Clean economics — clear reporting, margins and unit economics
  • Sensible pricing — value-based models that capture what you're worth
  • Commercial discipline — pipeline management and sales processes
  • Security & compliance uplift — ISO 27001, GDPR, public-sector standards
  • AI & engineering capability — embedded tech expertise, not consultants

Growth

Expand your market reach through the power of the platform.

  • Sell more to shared customers — cross-sell across the portfolio
  • Open new Nordic countries for proven products
  • Add adjacent workflows for the same buyer persona
  • Joint marketing & events to shared decision-makers
  • Warm introductions through trusted existing relationships

— This is by design. When founders stay invested, incentives stay aligned.
How we work with AI

AI is how we create value:
Every product, every workflow, every job role

We don't just talk about AI — we embed it across every company in the group. From new product capabilities to automating internal operations, AI is central to how PubliX creates value.

Product AI

We help our companies build AI-powered features into their products — from intelligent automation to predictive analytics for public-sector workflows.

Operational AI

We automate lead generation, lead nurture, customer support, software development and financial and administrative workflows across the group — so our companies can focus on building great products instead of admin.

We train every team across the group to use AI tools effectively — turning capability into results. Our companies reach their goals faster than they thought possible.
