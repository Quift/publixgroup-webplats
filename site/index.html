Struggling with long sales cycles?

Your customers are already our customers

When companies serving the same public-sector clients join the same group, something powerful happens: cross-selling becomes natural. Your scheduling platform is already deployed in the municipality that needs our grants management system.

Instead of competing for attention, our portfolio companies open doors for each other — shared customer relationships, joint marketing resources, and warm introductions that would take years to build alone.

Warm introductions

Skip the cold outreach — get introduced by a colleague the customer already trusts.

Losing tenders you should be winning?

We've run more public procurements than anyone

Public procurement is complex, bureaucratic and unforgiving. Miss a requirement, lose the deal. For a single company, each tender is a costly bet.

With multiple portfolio companies, PubliX runs more public tenders, across more municipalities, more often than any individual company could. That volume gives us a structural advantage — we know the process, we know the pitfalls, and we have dedicated resources to help you win.

Plus shared IT infrastructure, security auditing and compliance frameworks that meet the toughest public-sector standards.

  • Dedicated procurement specialists across the group
  • Shared compliance and IT security frameworks
  • Volume advantage: more tenders, more municipalities, more wins
  • Template library from hundreds of successful bids
  • Legal review and risk assessment for every submission
Worried about losing control of your company?

We don't change what works. We build on it.

When we acquire a company, we're not buying a product — we're investing in the people and organisation that built it. That value doesn't survive aggressive restructuring.

Every company in our group keeps its own CEO, its own team, its own office, and its own decision-making authority. We don't centralise functions to cut costs. We don't merge brands.

Instead, we invest in developing that existing value — through shared learning, cross-company competence building, and the kind of strategic support that helps founders focus on what they do best: building great products.

  • Your CEO stays. Full decision-making authority retained.
  • Your team stays. No layoffs, no "efficiency" programmes.
  • Your office stays. You operate from where you are.
  • Your brand stays. Your identity is part of your value.
  • Your culture stays. We add resources, not restrictions.

Ready to grow beyond your home market?

Take your product beyond the Nordics

Your product works in your home market. It could work across Europe. But international expansion requires market knowledge, legal expertise, and localisation capabilities that are hard to build from scratch.

PubliX already has pan-Nordic presence and is currently expanding into Northern Europe, the British and the DACH region. We bring market intelligence, legal frameworks, language capabilities and marketing expertise that make it possible for our companies to launch in new markets.

The European public sector is a massive, under-digitised market. We help you get there.
