A brand for the future of public services

Our visual system — navy-led, precise and human — built to present mission-critical public-sector data with clarity and trust.

The idea

Data, turning into progress

PubliX Group unites founder-led SaaS companies to modernise public services across the Nordics. The mark is built from pixel-squares that rise like a bar chart — data becoming momentum, the accelerator at work.

Everything else stays calm and squared-off: deep navy for authority, a single bright azure for action, and a disciplined palette engineered for charts and financial reporting.
