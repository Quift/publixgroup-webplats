Semantic status pill — application status, shift state, grant outcomes.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Grant paid</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" dot>Pending review</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger" icon="ph-flag">Flagged</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand">Social care</Badge>
```

- `tone`: `neutral` · `info` · `success` · `warning` · `danger` · `brand` · `solid`
- `dot` adds a status dot; `icon` takes a Phosphor class; `size`: `sm` · `md`
