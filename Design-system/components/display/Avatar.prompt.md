Avatar with photo or auto-initials from `name`.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Alexander Hübel" />
<Avatar name="Mattias Lind" src="/people/mattias.jpg" size="lg" />
<Avatar name="Tidvis" shape="square" tone="navy" />
```

- `size`: `xs` · `sm` · `md` · `lg` · `xl`; `shape`: `round` · `square`
- `tone`: `default` (navy-soft) · `navy` · `azure`
